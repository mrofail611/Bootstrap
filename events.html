Cape Fear Fair & Expo – October 28 – November 6, 2022

The Cape Fear Fair & Expo will return to the Wilmington International Airport in 2022 for its 57th annual event. Your kids will go wild over the family-friendly fair attractions and activities including educational agricultural magic shows, petting zoos, a Victorian-style circus, a comedy hypnotist and much more. Peruse judged exhibits in several categories including agriculture, arts and crafts, entomology, baked goods, livestock and more.
